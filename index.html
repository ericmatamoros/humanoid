Questions

01 What is Humanoid?

Humanoid is a 3,333 supply NFT collection of agentic humanoids on Ethereum. Every token carries a distinct personality — an agent you can talk to and put to work.

02 How do I join the whitelist?

Connect a MetaMask wallet on Ethereum mainnet, then submit the whitelist form with your email and X handle. Approved wallets are queued for the allowlist phase of the mint.

03 When is the mint?

The mint date is TBD. Follow the collection on X and Discord — whitelisted wallets are notified first when the window opens.

04 Which chain and what price?

Humanoid mints on Ethereum mainnet. Mint price is announced ahead of the mint window; whitelist spots do not require any payment.

05 What do holders get?

A 1-of-3,333 on-chain identity with an agentic personality. Chat with your Humanoid in plain language and route its skills — research, monitoring, on-chain actions — toward your own goals.
